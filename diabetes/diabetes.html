Diabetics

Diabetes in Singapore

Diabetes is becoming more and more common, accounting for 10% of all diseases in Singapore. People are becoming more sedentary and they have more sugar in their diets, so many people are at the risk of diabetes. We need to reduce the Diabetic population in Singapore.

How our features support their health

Salus can recommend healthier food for the diabetics that have less sugar in them, so that the diabetics' condition does not worsen. If the diabetic people have too much sugar in their diet, Salus can warn them to cut down on sugar and suggest healthier alternatives. Salus can also remind the diabetics to take their medication such as insulin.
