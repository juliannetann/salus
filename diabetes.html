Diabetic Population

Health of Diabetic People in Singapore

In Singapore, the number of people with diabetes is steadily increasing. In 2014, about 440,000 Singapore residents who were 18 years and above had diabetes, and the number is estimated to grow to 1,000,000 in 2050. Diabetes was the fourth and eighth most common condition of polyclinic attendances and hospitalisation respectively in 2014. Life years lost due to mortality and ill-health related to diabetes was the fourth largest among all diseases in 2010.
